BMW Information

History of BMW

The BMW company was founded on March 7, 1916, by Karl Rapp, Franz Josef Popp, and Gustav Otto. It originally produced aircraft engines.

The Best Sports Car

The BMW M5 CS is the fastest BMW sports car, with an acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h in 3 seconds. It features a V8M TwinPower Turbo engine with 625 horsepower and a maximum speed of 250 km/h.

The Best Exclusive Car

The BMW M8 GTR is one of BMW's exclusive cars, with a V8 M TwinPower Turbo engine and 625 horsepower. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in 3.2 seconds and has a top speed of 305 km/h.
